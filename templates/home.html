Instructions

Please read the instructions carefully

  • This is a demo website. Feel free to log in even if you are not from NIT Calicut.

  • If you are logging in for the first time, a new set of courses will be assigned to you.

  • You can check out the admin mode from the menu bar.

  • After submission, you cannot change your responses for that course.

  • Don't press back or reload button after logging in.

  • If you can't change your google account, try

    • Using incognito window.

    • Signing out from other google accounts.

    • Deleting cookies and clearing cache.
